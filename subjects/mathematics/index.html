Reason with confidence

Mathematics is not a collection of tricks to memorize. It is a way to describe structure, test ideas, and make careful arguments. This path emphasizes why methods work—not only how to use them.

01

Numbers & structure

Build strong intuition for quantities, operations, and relationships.

02

Algebraic thinking

Use symbols to express patterns and reason about the unknown.

03

Functions & change

Understand how quantities depend on and vary with one another.

04

Geometry & space

Explore shape, measurement, transformations, and proof.

05

Data & uncertainty

Draw responsible conclusions from variation and evidence.
