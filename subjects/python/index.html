Learn to speak to computers

Python is a powerful first programming language because its ideas are visible and its syntax stays out of the way. This learning path begins with expressions and data, then steadily builds toward programs that solve real problems.

01

Values & expressions

How programs represent information and produce results.

02

Variables & decisions

Give ideas names and teach programs to choose a path.

03

Loops & collections

Work effectively with sequences and repeated actions.

04

Functions

Break a complex problem into clear, reusable parts.

05

Files & data

Read, transform, and preserve information.

06

Build a project

Bring everything together in a program of your own.
