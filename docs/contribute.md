## Help build the campus

You do not need to be a professor—or even an expert—to make FreeCampus better. Clear feedback from a first-time learner can be as valuable as a new chapter from an experienced teacher.

<div class="contribution-grid">
  <div><span>Write</span><h3>Explain an idea</h3><p>Create lessons, examples, exercises, or a complete learning path in a subject you know well.</p></div>
  <div><span>Review</span><h3>Make it stronger</h3><p>Check material for accuracy, clarity, accessibility, and the questions a learner may still have.</p></div>
  <div><span>Build</span><h3>Improve the platform</h3><p>Help with the site, learning tools, documentation, testing, or other open infrastructure.</p></div>
  <div><span>Learn</span><h3>Share your experience</h3><p>Try a lesson, note where you hesitated, and help us see the material through fresh eyes.</p></div>
</div>

## How to begin

1. Visit the [FreeCampus organization on GitHub](https://github.com/freecampus).
2. Look through the open projects and choose an issue that matches your interests.
3. Introduce yourself, ask questions, and make a small first contribution.

Every contribution is reviewed collaboratively. We care about the quality of the work and the experience of the people creating it.
