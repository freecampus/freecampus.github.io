Help build the campus

You do not need to be a professor—or even an expert—to make FreeCampus better. Clear feedback from a first-time learner can be as valuable as a new chapter from an experienced teacher.

Write

Explain an idea

Create lessons, examples, exercises, or a complete learning path in a subject you know well.

Review

Make it stronger

Check material for accuracy, clarity, accessibility, and the questions a learner may still have.

Build

Improve the platform

Help with the site, learning tools, documentation, testing, or other open infrastructure.

Learn

Share your experience

Try a lesson, note where you hesitated, and help us see the material through fresh eyes.

How to begin

  1. Visit the FreeCampus organization on GitHub.
  2. Look through the open projects and choose an issue that matches your interests.
  3. Introduce yourself, ask questions, and make a small first contribution.

Every contribution is reviewed collaboratively. We care about the quality of the work and the experience of the people creating it.
